About me

I received my Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in 2020. I am currently an assistant researcher in the School of Computer and Information, Hohai University, China. My research interests mainly include memory system, storage system, and operating system. Particularly, my recent works focus on efficient architecture supports and high-performance system software for Non-Volatile Memory (NVM) spanning file systems, NoSQL databases, and key-value stores. My future works explore next-generation storage systems for resource disaggregation with network-storage co-design.
